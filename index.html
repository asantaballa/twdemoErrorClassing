    The "what"

    This is a demo of how to use raw SQL for classifying a set of text log or error messages.

    The "why"

    Sometimes the logging of errors and events is not well thought out as to how to group types of entries. You come in after the fact to do the analysis and need a quick way to make sense of a large number of entries.
